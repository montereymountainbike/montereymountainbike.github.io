Our Story

Born from a Love of
the Open Trail

Monterey Mountain Bike Tours was founded by a lifelong cyclist with a passion for sharing the natural beauty of California's central coast. What started as weekend rides with friends grew into Monterey's most loved bike adventure company.

We take pride in our top-quality equipment, safety-first approach, and expert guides who know every corner of these incredible trails. Whether it's your first time on a mountain bike or you're a seasoned rider — we make every ride unforgettable.

Top-of-the-line mountain bikes, constantly maintained
